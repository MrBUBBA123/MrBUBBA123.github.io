Narrative Visualization to Study the Impact of the NFL's Player Safety Initiatives for Concussions

The NFL previously ignored decades of research on the degenerative long-term effects of brain trauma, and didn’t take enough steps to protect its employees. Thousands of now-settled lawsuits brought on by former players forced the NFL to change their ways. Through this narrative visualization, we dive into the correlation between the NFL’s increased safety measures and the number of concussions spanning the last decade.

Data Source: NFL Concussion Data
ℹ️ Important Context: NFL Season Structure Changes

2015-2019: 4 Preseason Games and 16 Regular Season Games per Team

2020: 0 Preseason Games and 16 Regular Season Games (Covid-19)

2021-2024: 3 Preseason Games and 17 Regular Season Games per Team
